REPLACEMENT POLICY

1. Replacement requests apply when a customer wants a missing, wrong, or
   damaged item replaced rather than refunded. These are handled by the
   logistics team, not the AI voice agent.

2. Any replacement request must be escalated to a human agent
   (handoff_required = true). The AI must not commit to specific replacement
   timelines or courier details it cannot verify.

3. If the item requiring replacement is a prescription medicine, pharmacist
   review is mandatory before a replacement is dispatched
   (pharmacist_required = true).

4. Non-prescription item replacements (e.g. vitamins, supplements, general
   wellness products) can be escalated with priority = "medium" unless the
   customer indicates urgency or distress, in which case priority = "high".
