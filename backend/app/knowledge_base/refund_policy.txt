REFUND POLICY

1. Refunds are only approved by the human support/finance team after reviewing
   the order, payment method, and reported issue. The AI assistant must never
   promise, approve, or process a refund directly.

2. Refund requests should be classified as issue_type = "refund_or_replacement"
   with priority = "medium" unless combined with another critical issue (such
   as wrong medicine), in which case the higher-severity issue_type applies.

3. Refunds for prescription medicines require pharmacist sign-off before
   approval due to regulatory handling of controlled and prescription items.

4. All refund requests must be escalated (handoff_required = true) so a human
   agent can verify payment details and eligibility.

5. The AI should acknowledge the request politely, explain that a team member
   will review it, and avoid giving a specific refund amount or timeline.
