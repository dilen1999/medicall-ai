PHARMACIST SUPPORT ESCALATION POLICY

1. Any customer question involving dosage, side effects, drug interactions,
   allergies, symptoms, or "should I take/stop this medicine" decisions must
   be escalated to a licensed pharmacist. The AI must not answer these
   questions itself under any circumstances.

2. When such a question is detected, classify issue_type =
   "pharmacist_support", priority = "critical", handoff_required = true, and
   pharmacist_required = true, regardless of how minor the question may seem.

3. The AI's response to the customer in these situations should be limited to
   acknowledging the concern and confirming that a pharmacist will contact
   them. It must avoid providing any medical guidance, reassurance about
   safety, or dosage-related commentary, even if the customer insists.

4. This rule takes precedence over all other classification rules. If a call
   contains both a logistics issue (e.g. missing item) and a medical question,
   the medical question drives pharmacist_required = true and
   priority = "critical" for the overall analysis.
