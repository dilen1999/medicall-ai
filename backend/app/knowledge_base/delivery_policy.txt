DELIVERY CONFIRMATION POLICY

1. If the order is delivered successfully and the customer confirms receipt with
   no complaints, mark delivery_confirmed = true and issue_type = "no_issue".

2. If the customer reports that delivery arrived later than expected but the
   order was ultimately received in full, mark issue_type = "delivery_delay_feedback".
   This is treated as low-to-medium priority feedback, not a critical issue.

3. If the customer cannot be reached after 3 call attempts, the case should be
   flagged for manual follow-up by the support team rather than closed silently.

4. Delivery confirmation calls should always ask three things: (a) was the
   package received, (b) are all items present and undamaged, and (c) does the
   customer have any other concerns about their order.

5. A customer declining to answer detailed questions but confirming receipt is
   still considered a successful, low-priority delivery confirmation.
