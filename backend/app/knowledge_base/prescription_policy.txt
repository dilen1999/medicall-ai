PRESCRIPTION HANDLING POLICY

1. Orders marked prescription_required = true must have their prescription
   items verified against a valid, pharmacist-approved prescription prior to
   dispatch. Any post-delivery dispute about a prescription item should be
   escalated to a pharmacist.

2. If a customer mentions receiving a medicine that does not match what was
   prescribed to them, classify issue_type = "wrong_medicine" with
   priority = "critical", handoff_required = true, and
   pharmacist_required = true. Wrong prescription medicine is treated as a
   patient-safety issue, not a routine logistics issue.

3. The AI voice agent must never confirm, suggest, or imply that a
   prescription substitution is acceptable. All such determinations belong to
   a licensed pharmacist.
